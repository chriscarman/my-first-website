My Apps

You can also find my own apps on this website, which were given as projects during the course. Most of them are quite basic and were mainly used to show how the Django files work together and communicate with the database.
The final project for the course was the Ads app, which allows to upload an ad for an item with a price, description and a photo. You can favorite ads and look up ad names if you are looking for one in particular. If you try clicking on everything, you might find a fun page.
All my apps are available in the 'My Implementations' menu.
